Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

DNR Adblocker ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your data. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The developer has disclosed that it will not collect or use your data.

DNR Adblocker operates entirely locally on your device. We do not track your browsing history, we do not store your personal information on any remote servers, and we do not transmit any of your data to ourselves or any third party.

2. Data Protection Commitments

In accordance with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, we declare that your data is:

  • Not being sold to third parties, outside of the approved use cases.
  • Not being used or transferred for purposes that are unrelated to the item's core functionality.
  • Not being used or transferred to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

3. Local Storage

The extension uses browser-provided local storage APIs (such as chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.session) solely to function correctly. This includes storing:

  • Your preferred settings (e.g., whether the extension is paused).
  • Your custom filtering rules.
  • Aggregated statistics (counters) of blocked requests.

This data remains on your local machine and is never accessible by the developer.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted directly on this page.
